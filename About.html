About Tiger

I’m a Graphic and Front End Designer, study, live and be inspired in NYC.

Love pixel work, love code and the most amazing part is to blend and mix them together.

I currently study at Pratt Institute, I enjoy the intense pace of my course there, and hungrily hunting other advance design trends and skills out of the campus.
